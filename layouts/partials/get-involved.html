Get Involved

ApacheIoT is a great opportunity to present what you have been working on to an engaged audience.

All talks should be submitted to the ApacheCon North America (2017) event with an ApacheIoT note.

Please specify ApacheIoT in the Audience section so the talk is added to the ApacheIoT tracks.

Each accepted talk includes one free registration to the conference.

The Call For Participation (CFP) closes on February 11th, so get your topic in today.
